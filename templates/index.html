Welcome to the Warehouse Safety Detection.

Warehouse safety is a set of regulatory guidelines and industry best practices to help warehousing personnel ensure a safe work environment and reinforce safe behavior when working in warehouses. For sustainable warehouse operations, health and safety should be prioritized as the Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA) revealed that the fatal injury rate for the warehousing industry is higher than the national average for all industries. This model can detect the safety equipments and trained with Yolo v7. The mPA of this model is around 87% and still learning.
